Unleaving by Orangutan Matter Games

Synopsis

It is a game looking to bring players into a thoughtful journey as they travel through a surreal world, exploring philosophical themes and metaphor. Bringing acrylic painting along with pencil and ink drawings to life, the players through Unleaving - a child - encounter the meaning and beauty (and despair) that is life.

Features

  • 2D physics platforming puzzles
  • Hand painting animation art!
  • No unnecessary bloating art, music, audio, text, or game mechanics.
    Just a pure minimalist approach to what matters for an emotional engagement.

Wishlist Unleaving

Early Beginnings

The inception of this game came from the idea; simple is beautiful. A young child finds every new experience exciting no matter how simple it is. In today's world where fast gratification is the norm, as adults our sense of wonder gets desensitized and we forget that the slow moments in life are still intriguing with their goods and bads. From that philosophy came the idea for Unleaving (previously named Jack), here is how it starts: A child ventures into a surreal world seeking purpose for his existence

The rest of the story goes.... well we'll leave the experience to you!
